Principal Message

At NES International School Mumbai-an IB World School, we are committed to support our parents and students through every leg of their journey. NESISM being the flagship of the highly regarded NES & SVB group of institutions is fully authorised as an International Baccalaureate Continuum World School and offers the IB Curriculum from Kindergarten through to Grade 12. The IB is widely regarded as the most prestigious programme of study available and offered in over 5,000 schools across the world.

Academics

he Learning Support Programme at NESISM comprises a team of qualified Counsellor, Special Educator and EAL Educator (English as an additional language) and believes in the importance of a collaborative approach to serving students, including parent, classroom teachers and administrators in implementing and monitoring for growth and success of the learner. The Learning support team work towards collaborative teaching approaches to motivate the learner to reach his maximum potential. The Learning Support team supports the school’s policy of promoting inclusive educational practice by collaborating at various levels. The LSP provides students with skills and strategies based on their Individualized Education Plan (IEP) to enhance their learning. The school community is sensitized about the needs of the learners, their individual strengths and challenges, and recommends differentiation strategies for learner’s learning needs and styles.
